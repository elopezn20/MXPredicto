## What is this?

A prediction pool for the FIFA World Cup 2026. Each player predicts the exact score of all 104 matches before each deadline. The player who accumulates the most points by the end of the tournament wins.

---

## How to join?

The pool is **invite-only**. Admins will send you an email with a registration link. That link takes you to a page where you choose your name and password. Once registered, you can log in at any time from the link above.

Each person may have at most one account — duplicate sign-ups are not allowed.

## How does predicting work?

### Match predictions

- For each match you predict the **exact score** (e.g. Argentina 2 – Brazil 1).
- In knockout matches where the score is level after regular time/extra time, you must also pick **who advances** (the penalty winner).
- You can save and update your predictions as many times as you like **before the deadline**.

### Round deadline

> **The round closes at 00:00 Chile time on the day of the first match in that round.**

If the first match of a round is on Monday, June 15 (Chile time), all predictions for that round are locked at 00:00 (midnight) on Monday, June 15 Chile time — even though the matches themselves are played later that day.

Once closed, predictions cannot be edited or added. Matches with no prediction are worth 0 points. The app shows a countdown to the next deadline.

### Bonus Podium

- You pick the **Champion** (50 pts), **Runner-up** (25 pts), and **Third place** (15 pts) of the World Cup. Maximum total: **90 pts**. Each slot is scored independently — get the Champion right and you earn 50 pts even if the other two are wrong.
- The three teams must be different.
- The window is open **during the group stage** (from the first day of the tournament). Once the first Round of 32 match kicks off, the Bonus Podium closes permanently.
- You can save and update your picks as many times as you like **before the window closes**.

## Points per match

### Group stage (max. 10 pts per match)

| Criterion | Points |
|---|---|
| Correct result (who wins or draw) | 5 |
| Exact home goals | 2 |
| Exact away goals | 2 |
| Correct goal difference (in the predicted direction) | 1 |

Points are cumulative. Perfect prediction = **10 pts**. Exact home and away goals earn points even if the overall result is wrong.

> **About goal difference:** The difference must match in both magnitude *and* direction. If you predict 2–1 (home wins by 1), you don't earn this point if the result is 1–2 or 2–3, even though the difference is also 1 — because the winner is different.

**Examples:**

| Prediction | Actual result | Pts | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2–1 | 2–1 | **10** | Perfect — result ✓ (5), home ✓ (2), away ✓ (2), diff+winner ✓ (1) |
| 2–0 | 3–1 | **6** | Result ✓ (5) + diff+winner ✓ (1); goals don't match |
| 1–1 | 2–2 | **6** | Draw ✓ (5) + diff 0 ✓ (1); goals don't match |
| 3–1 | 2–1 | **7** | Result ✓ (5) + exact away ✓ (2); home wrong, diff different |
| 1–0 | 1–0 | **10** | Perfect |
| 2–1 | 2–0 | **7** | Result ✓ (5), home ✓ (2), away ✗; diff different (1 ≠ 2) |
| 0–1 | 0–2 | **7** | Result ✓ (5), home ✓ (2), away ✗; diff different |
| 1–1 | 1–0 | **2** | Result ✗ (predicted draw, home won); home ✓ (2); away ✗ |
| 2–1 | 1–2 | **0** | Wrong result — nothing scores |
| 0–0 | 0–0 | **10** | Perfect |

> **Note on draws:** If you predict a draw and the match ends in a draw, you earn the goal difference bonus point even if the goals are different (both have difference 0 and "winner" = draw).

### Knockout (max. 25 pts per match)

| Criterion | Points |
|---|---|
| Correct result after regular time or extra time | 10 |
| Exact home goals | 4 |
| Exact away goals | 4 |
| Correct goal difference (in the predicted direction) | 2 |
| Who advances | 5 |

Perfect prediction = **25 pts**.

> **About the score:** Extra time goals count in the final score. If the match is 1–1 at 90' and the home team scores in extra time, the official result is **2–1**. Penalties only apply if the score is still level after extra time.

> **Goals independent of result:** Hitting the home or away goal tally earns points even if the overall result is wrong.

> **About goal difference:** The difference must match in both magnitude *and* direction. If you predict 2–1 (home wins by 1), you don't earn this criterion if the result is 1–2 or 2–3, even though the difference is also 1 — because the winner is different.

> **Who advances — regardless of how:** This criterion only depends on which team progressed to the next round, regardless of whether it was in regular time, extra time, or penalties. Your predicted team is compared directly with who advanced — if they match, you earn the 5 points even if the exact score was different.

**Examples:**

| Prediction | Actual result | Advances | Pts | Breakdown |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2–1 home | 2–1 home | Home | **25** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✓4, diff✓2, adv✓5 |
| 0–2 away | 0–2 away | Away | **25** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✓4, diff✓2, adv✓5 |
| 1–1 + home | 1–1 | Home | **25** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✓4, diff✓2, adv✓5 |
| 1–1 + home | 1–1 | Away | **20** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✓4, diff✓2, adv✗0 |
| 1–1 + (no pick) | 1–1 | Home | **20** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✓4, diff✓2, adv✗0 — must pick penalty winner |
| 2–0 home | 2–1 home | Home | **19** | Res✓10, home✓4, away✗0, diff✗0, adv✓5 |
| 3–0 home | 1–0 home | Home | **19** | Res✓10, home✗0, away✓4, diff✗0, adv✓5 |
| 1–0 home | 2–1 home | Home | **17** | Res✓10, home✗0, away✗0, diff✓2, adv✓5 |
| 2–1 home | 1–1 + away | Away | **4** | Res✗0, home✗0, away✓4, diff✗0, adv✗0 |
| 2–1 home | 1–1 + home | Home | **9** | Res✗0, home✗0, away✓4, diff✗0, adv✓5 — predicted home winner, home advanced |
| 1–1 + home | 2–1 home | Home | **9** | Res✗0, home✗0, away✓4, diff✗0, adv✓5 — penalty pick matches who advanced |
| 1–1 + home | 1–0 home | Home | **9** | Res✗0, home✓4, away✗0, diff✗0, adv✓5 — penalty pick matches who advanced |
| 2–1 home | 0–2 away | Away | **0** | Res✗0, home✗0, away✗0, diff✗0, adv✗0 |
| 0–1 away | 1–0 home | Home | **0** | Res✗0, home✗0, away✗0, diff✗0, adv✗0 |

| Criterion | Points |

Each position is scored independently. You can earn 50 pts just for the Champion even if the rest is wrong.

## Standings

The ranking is ordered by **total points** (matches + podium).

**Tiebreaker (in order):**
1. Most "hits" — matches scored at maximum: **10 pts** in group stage, **25 pts** in knockout.
2. Fewest matches with 0 points ("zeros") — matches with no prediction count as zero. Partial scores (>0 but below the match maximum) are neither hits nor zeros.

The table shows: rank, name, points, hits, zeros, and gap to the leader.

## Prediction visibility

- Your predictions are **private until the round closes**.
- Once the round closes, everyone can see everyone's picks for those matches.
- Your **Bonus Podium** is likewise private until the Podium window closes (when the first Round of 32 match begins). After that, everyone can see everyone's podium picks.

## FAQ

**Can I change my prediction?**
Yes, as many times as you want before the round closes. The last saved version is the one that counts.

**What if I don't predict a match?**
That match is worth 0 points for you.

**What about the Bonus Podium?**
You can save and update your picks as many times as you want before the window closes. The last saved version is the one that counts.

**How do I know when each round closes?**
The app shows a countdown at all times. Each round's deadline is 00:00 Chile time on the day of the first match in that round — when the matchday begins, not when its matches actually kick off.
